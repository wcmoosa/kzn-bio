About Us

Our Services

Comprehensive biokinetic solutions for optimal health and performance.

Stroke and Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialized rehabilitation programs to help patients recover from strokes and neurological conditions, focusing on regaining mobility, strength, and independence.

Post-Accident and Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs for motor vehicle accident recovery and post-surgical rehabilitation, helping patients safely return to daily activities.

Chronic Pain and Joint Condition Management

Evidence-based exercise therapy programs designed to manage chronic pain, improve joint function, and enhance quality of life for those with ongoing conditions.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation and Conditioning

Targeted rehabilitation and conditioning programs for athletes recovering from sports injuries, focusing on strength reconditioning and safe return to sport.

Exercise Therapy for Medical Conditions

Individualized exercise prescriptions for various medical conditions, helping patients manage symptoms, improve function, and enhance overall health outcomes.

Corporate Wellness and Injury Prevention Programmes

Workplace wellness initiatives and preventative exercise programs designed to reduce injury risk, improve employee health, and enhance workplace productivity.

About KZN Biokinetics

Professional Rehabilitation & Wellness in KwaZulu-Natal

20+ Years of Excellence

KZN Biokinetics is a well-established biokinetics practice in Westville, Durban, with over 20 years of clinical experience delivering evidence-based rehabilitation, injury management, and movement therapy to the KwaZulu-Natal community.

Founded by Deshen Naidoo

BHSc Hons Biokinetics, UKZN

KZN Biokinetics has been at the forefront of biokinetic rehabilitation since 2002. Deshen has a strong clinical focus on stroke rehabilitation, post-accident recovery, and chronic condition management, helping patients safely regain strength, mobility, and independence. His patient-centred approach emphasises functional recovery, long-term wellness, and restoring quality of life.

Senior Biokineticist: Illonka Eayrs

BHSc Biokinetics, Nelson Mandela University

Illonka brings experience across the Eastern Cape and KwaZulu-Natal. She is actively involved in both individual and corporate wellness initiatives and is passionate about delivering a dynamic, preventative approach to rehabilitation and performance care.
